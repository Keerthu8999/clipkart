Verdict
The Coolpad Note 5 is a awesome handset which packs in quality elements. The built up of this set is very good for multitasking and high end gaming. Both the processors outputs quality performance and the cameras also captures great shots. Selfie lovers can also take nice pics in low light due to the presence of front flash. Other setup of this phone such as fast charging, ample battery backup, fingerprint scanner, OTG cable support, removable battery etc makes this phone very worthy, you can go for it.
An outstanding performer with front flash

Display and Configuration
The Coolpad Note 5 comes with a 5.5-inch IPS LCD Full HD (1,080 x 1,920 pixels) display and has a pixel density of 401ppi. It measures 152 x 75.7 x 8.8mm, has a metal body and is available in two coloured variants - royal gold and space black. Under the hood, there is are two quad-core processors (1.5GHz Cortex A53 + 1.2GHz Cortex A53) aided by 4GB of RAM and Adreno 405 graphics engine seated upon Qualcomm Snapdragon 617 MSM8952 chipset. The device boots on Android v6.0.1 (Marshmallow) operating system (Cool UI 8.0) and is embedded by a fingerprint scanner at the back. 

Camera and Storage
The device is armed by a 13MP camera (f/2.2 aperture) at the back and a 8MP (front flash) snapper at the front for selfie lovers. The main camera features Digital Zoom, Auto Flash, Face detection, Touch to focus etc. It also allows the users to record Full HD 1080p videos and share it with their close ones. On the storage ground, the device houses an internal memory of 32GB which can be expanded upto more 64GB. One can increase the memory more further by using OTG cable. 

Battery and Connectivity
The device is fuelled by a 4,010mAh li-polymer battery (inclusive of quick charging feature) which claims to render a talktime upto 200 hours. For networking and sharing, the device offers options namely - dual SIM, 4G (supports Indian bands), 3G, 2G, VoLTE, Wi-Fi 802.11, b/g/n, Mobile Hotspot, Bluetooth v4.0, GPS with A-GPS and microUSB 2.0.
